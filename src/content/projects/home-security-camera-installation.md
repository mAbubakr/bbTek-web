## Project Overview

Designed and installed a complete security system for a residential property — covering every entry point and key interior areas, with full remote access from the client's phone.

## The Challenge

The homeowner had experienced a break-in attempt at a neighbouring property and wanted peace of mind. Their existing setup was a single outdated doorbell camera with no recording, no alerts, and no interior coverage.

## What Was Done

**Camera System**
- 8-camera exterior coverage (front, rear, both side yards, garage, driveway)
- 4 interior cameras (entry hall, living room, garage interior, basement)
- Night-vision and weatherproof units rated for Canadian winters
- Local NVR with 4TB storage plus cloud backup for critical clips

**Alarm Integration**
- Wired contact sensors on all ground-floor doors and windows
- Motion detectors covering the main floor and basement
- Siren with interior and exterior alerts
- Full integration with camera system — alarm triggers camera recording and push notification

**Mobile App Setup**
- Configured remote viewing through a unified app for both cameras and alarm
- Set up customized alert zones and schedules to minimize false notifications
- Walked the client through the full interface and controls

## Results

- Complete coverage of the property with no blind spots
- Real-time alerts sent within 2 seconds of motion or alarm trigger
- Client can arm, disarm, and review footage from anywhere
- System has been live for 6+ months with zero downtime

## Technologies Used

- Hikvision NVR and IP camera system
- DSC alarm panel
- Hik-Connect mobile platform
- Structured cabling with PoE switches

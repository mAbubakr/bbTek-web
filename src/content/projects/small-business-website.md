## Project Overview

Built a full website for a local service business that needed a professional online presence — something they could actually maintain themselves after launch, and that would show up in local search results.

## The Challenge

The client was running their business entirely on social media and word-of-mouth. They had no website, no way for customers to book online, and zero search visibility. A competitor in the same area had a polished site and was picking up work they should have been getting.

## What Was Delivered

**Design & Layout**
- Clean, modern design matched to their brand colours and voice
- Fully mobile-responsive — tested across phones, tablets, and desktop
- Service pages for each offering with clear calls to action
- About page that builds trust with their story and credentials

**Booking & Contact**
- Integrated booking form with availability calendar
- Automated email confirmations to both client and customer
- Contact form with spam filtering

**SEO & Performance**
- Local SEO setup: Google Business Profile integration, structured data, and location-targeted copy
- Page load under 1.5s on mobile
- Meta titles, descriptions, and Open Graph tags configured for every page

**CMS for Self-Management**
- Simple content management interface so the owner can update services, pricing, and photos without touching code
- Training session included so they're confident managing it themselves

## Results

- Site launched in 3 weeks from initial brief
- Ranked on the first page of Google for their primary service keyword within 6 weeks
- Booking form generating 5–10 new customer inquiries per week within 2 months
- Client has updated their own content multiple times without needing help

## Technologies Used

- Astro (static site with CMS integration)
- Tailwind CSS
- Netlify for hosting and form handling
- Google Search Console and Analytics
